Island House - bringing the outside in

Newly refurbished, Island House has outstanding views and all the comforts of home. The house is an ideal base from which to explore Lindisfarne.

Let us show you around…

Lounge

Flooded with light. Sliding French windows offer superb sea views across to the far shoreline. Relax on the comfy sofas and enjoy the view or bring out the garden furniture and enjoy a glass of chilled wine on the sunny private West South West patio as the evening sun sets. In the cool of autumn and winter cosy up in front of the open fire with a book, or enjoy all of the necessary mod cons – a Wi-Fi router, Bluetooth Stereo System, TV and DVD player.

Study

A book, a cup of tea, the North Sea. Off the lounge, find our cosy library, filled with hundreds of books. Read a book and watch the clouds pass by, or train your telescope onto the seals on the sandbanks and sanderlings skittering in and out of the wake, through the floor-to-ceiling window.

Dining kitchen

With panoramic views. The large modern kitchen offers a dishwasher, double oven, microwave and all the necessary cooking equipment you would expect to find. Sliding glass doors open onto the patio and garden, sip your breakfast coffee or enjoy your evening meal listening to the waves lapping the shore, whilst the seals sit singing on the sandbanks.
By day watch the tides change and see the birds come flocking in, and on a clear night enjoy a starlit sky and a moonlit sea.

Double Bedroom

The downstairs bedroom features a comfortable pocket-sprung 4’6” double bed, bedside reading lamps, storage space, and a hairdryer. Not forgetting, pure cotton sheets, down duvets and down pillows which offer sumptuous comfort (in every bedroom).

King size en-suite bedroom

This spacious bedroom offers wonderful sea views over to the mainland, the causeway, Cuthbert’s Island and across the sand dunes to the Cheviot Hills. The pocket-sprung king-size bed offers a luxurious night’s sleep, there is ample storage, reading lamps and a hairdryer.

En-suite bathroom

Enjoy a relaxing bath in the smart en-suite, with two raised windows offering views across the shore and the far distant lights of Berwick by night.

Twin bedroom

Two comfortable pocket sprung 3’6” single beds offer flexible sleeping arrangements, suitable for adults and children alike. Like the other bedrooms, the twin room offers storage, bedside lamp and a hairdryer. This bedroom also enjoys fantastic vistas over the sea.

House bathroom

In addition to the en-suite bathroom in the king-size double bedroom and the downstairs WC, there is a House Bathroom. This is a fully tiled, walk-in wet room, with under floor heating and a towel warmer for quick drying.

Garden

The lawn-covered rear garden is West South West facing, and completely private. It offers fabulous views and is sunny from mid-morning until the sun sets. Help yourself to fresh herbs snipped from the herb bed outside the kitchen door.

General

Parking is available for up to three cars in Island House’s private drive. A private, secure yard to the side of the house houses the coal bunker and provides a safe storage space for bikes.

The front entrance lobby has a useful coat cupboard and boot rack to dry out any wet gear, and a downstairs WC.

An automatic washing machine and separate tumble dryer make doing any washing easy. The extra fridge and a freezer provide useful additional food storage space.

The house is fully double-glazed throughout, and features oil-fired central heating as well as an open fire, fuel provided.

Unfortunately Island House is unable to accept animal guests.

Island House is a no smoking property.
