Welcome to Island House

Newly refurbished with all the comforts of home, full oil fired central heating, double glazing.

House Tour

Come in, relax and enjoy the outstanding sea views! On your arrival at the house park up to three cars in our private drive. From the front door enter into the lobby with a useful coat cupboard, shoe and boot racks and cloakroom WC.

Sitting room

The light filled sitting room has a superb view of the sea with French windows to the garden and terrace.Relax on the comfortable sofas, and toast your toes in front of the open fire. A wifi router, Bluetooth stereo system, TV DVD are provided for you to keep up with the world.

Library

Off the lounge is a cosy library with hundreds of books. A perfect cosy space to discover your new favourite read, or simply to snuggle down and watch the seals on the sandbanks.

Dining kitchen

With spectacular panoramic views, watch the tides change and do a spot of bird watching from the spacious, well equipped, dining kitchen with dishwasher, double oven and hob and all the equipment you could need. The floor length windows with huge sliding doors lead to the terrace and private sunny garden.

Utility room

A washer and separate tumble dryer, extra fridge and freezer, ironing board make it easy to do any laundry and store extra food supplies.

Downstairs Double Bedroom

A cosy room with a comfortable pocket sprung 4’6”double bed. An open tread staircase leads from the lounge to the first floor with views of The Priory and St Mary’s Church from the landing.

King size en-suite bedroom

Fabulous panoramic sea views to the mainland, causeway, St Cuthbert’s Island, Cheviot Hills and sand dunes. Sink into the king size pocket sprung bed and enjoy the peace and quiet of Holy Island. Ensuite bathroom with Villeroy and Boch suite.

Twin bedroom

With two comfortable 3’6” single beds and fantastic panoramic sea views to the causeway, mainland and Cheviot Hills. All bedrooms have ample clothes storage and hairdryers. The comfortable pocket sprung beds are made up with pure cotton bedding, down duvets and a selection of down or hypoallergenic pillows.

House bathroom

A luxurious fully tiled walk in wet room with underfloor heating and towel warmer.

Garden

The west south west facing private, well stocked lawned rear garden has fabulous panoramic sea views and is sunny from mid-morning until the sun sets. Help yourselves to fresh herbs in the herb bed, relax and enjoy the views and the sunsets. A private secure yard to the side of the house houses the coal bunker and provides a safe storage space for bikes.

General

The house has full oil fired central heating and is fully double glazed.
