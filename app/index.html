Coble Cottage Offers Some of the most spectacular views

and every aspect has been designed to make the most of them.

Architect-designed, newly built and equipped to the highest specification throughout, the spacious open-plan living and dining area is blessed with outstanding natural light and stunning uninterrupted views of Lindisfarne Castle.

Here you can sink into the luxurious sofas, cook up a storm in a kitchen equipped for every eventuality and enjoy a meal with friends and family at a dining table which comfortably seats 10.

The 4 bedrooms sleep 8 and all have ensuite bath or shower rooms. There are two double rooms, a twin bedded room and a room containing bunk beds, and a cot can be accommodated in one of the bedrooms on request.

Coble Cottage combines the best home comforts with 5* luxury; we hope you won't want to leave.
